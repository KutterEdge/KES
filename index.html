About Kutter Edge

First of all, allow me to introduce myself, My name is Keith, but most people online call me Kutter.

I am a dad first so getting to game is a fun hobby for me. I try to livestream if I can but love to just get on the PC and game with friends. Like all people on the internet, I started out watching and enjoying videos of people playing video games.

So I saved for almost a year and bought a computer that could handle almost any game on the market. In 2008, I started buying parts and got my first PC build together, after that it was "Game On".

I like playing a lot of different games and I'm not picky one over the other. I now have started working in graphic designs and dabbling in a little coding here and there.
